Websit Brief

ReduceFoodWaste is a website dedicated to tackling the global food waste problem. Our mission is to raise awareness about the devastating environmental, social, and economic impacts of food waste and to provide individuals, businesses, and communities with strategies and resources to reduce waste. By working together, we can make a difference and create a more sustainable and equitable food system. Join us now to help solve the global food waste problem!
